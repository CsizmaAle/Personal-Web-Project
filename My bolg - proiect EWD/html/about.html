About Me

Hi, I'm Alexandra! I'm a first-year computer science student with a passion for challenges, video games, and stepping out of my comfort zone. I love learning as much as possible, whether it's about programming, mathematical logic, or game development. I have experience with C/C++, C#, and Python, and I'm currently exploring Unity and CustomTkinter for creating projects and video games. Creativity is a big part of who I am, and I enjoy expressing it through coding, reading, and painting when I have the time. Oh, and I’m also a coffee enthusiast!

Blog Description

Welcome to Alexandra - Sofia Csizmadia - My Blog, a space where code meets creativity! As a computer science student and an art enthusiast, I share my journey through the challenges and triumphs of student life—balancing algorithms, debugging sessions, and exams with moments of artistic expression. Whether it's exploring programming concepts, tackling real-world projects, or diving into the world of painting and design, this blog is all about the intersection of logic and creativity. Join me as I navigate the tech world while embracing the beauty of art!
